Educational Resources#

Open Educational Resources (OER)#

  • OER Commons: a public digital library of open educational resources.

  • MIT Open Courseware: videos and course materials for full courses at MIT

  • Open Yale Courses: full courses from Yale University

  • LabXchange: free science content, through a collaboration including Harvard, Amgen, Khan Academy, and more.

  • SkillsCommons: open library of workforce training materials, supported by the MERLOT project at CSU Long Beach.

Public Domain works#

  • Project Gutenberg: online library of public domain works

  • Standard Ebooks: open source public domain works with high quality formatting

  • Library Athena: another collection of public domain works, nicely formatted

  • Internet Sacred Texts Archive: a large freely available archive of online books about religion, mythology, and folklore.

  • Cookbooks: thousands of archived cookbooks

Open Textbooks#

There are lots of free and open source textbooks online. Some books are made available by the author, and others by the publisher.

  • OpenStax: peer-reviewed free textbooks published by Rice University

  • Open Textbook Library: searchable collection of open textbooks hosted by University of Minnesota

  • Open Textbooks: searchable collection from OER Commons

  • OASIS: open educational resource database hosted by Geneseo college (SUNY)

  • Online Books Page: search engine from UPenn

  • Open Book Publishers: an independent open access academic publisher

  • LibreTexts: open, interactive, online textbooks

Math and Computer Science Textbooks#

  • Real Not Complex: a large collection of free math textbooks from elementary school through graduate-level mathematics

  • American Institute of Mathematics: open textbooks approved by the American Institute of Mathematics

  • Green Tea Press: publisher of free computer science books

  • Open Content Mathematics Curriculum: a selection of books for a full undergraduate mathematics curriculum, from Professor Robert Beezer

  • Free and Open-Source Textbooks: another list of free college-level mathematics textbooks from Professor Dana Ernst

  • Springer: graduate level math and physics books available during the pandemic

  • Paulâ€™s Online Notes: free online and downloadable math notes and tutorials

  • Computer Science Textbooks: a list of CS books from Professor Colin S. Gordon

Reference#

  • NIST Digital Library of Mathematical Functions: mathematical function reference from the National Institute of Standards and Technology

  • The Mathematical Functions Grimoire: open source library of formulas and data for special functions
